The Office, is an American sitcom created by Ricky Gervais and Stephen Merchant broadcast on the NBC network between March 24, 2005 and May 16, 2013.
It spans nine seasons and 201 episodes lasting from around twenty-two minutes to over forty minutes for some episodes.
